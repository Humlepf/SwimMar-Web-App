Swim-Mar is a free scheduling tool created exclusively for outdoor swimming. Using Swim-Mar, you will be able to create and join swimming sessions with other swimmers in your local area.

A better way to swim outdoors.

  • Accurate Swim Calendars

    Up-to-date sessions posted by local swimmers.

  • Easy Sign-Up

    Log on and instantly join local sessions that interest you.

  • Swim Maps and Directories

    Discover new places to swim, vetted and maintained by local admins.

  • Cross-Device Compatibility

    Works great on your desktop, smartphone or tablet.
